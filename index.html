Our Story

Crafting Comfort,
One Piece at a Time

Founded in 2023 by master craftsman Victor, Remedy Upholstery has been helping clients transform worn, outdated, and damaged furniture into beautifully renewed pieces. Based in Bramley, Johannesburg, we blend timeless upholstery techniques with a refined modern finish.

Every project starts with a conversation. We take time to understand your vision, your style, and the way you live, then bring it all together with quality materials, expert workmanship, and careful attention to detail.

Whether you are restoring a treasured family piece, upgrading your home interior, or commissioning custom upholstery for a new space, we are committed to delivering workmanship you can see and comfort you can feel.
