Our Story

Crafting Comfort,
One Piece at a Time

Founded in 2023 by master craftsman Victor, Remedy Upholstery has been transforming worn furniture into heirloom-quality pieces. Located in Bramely Johannesburg, we combine old-world technique with modern design sensibility.

Every project begins with a conversation. We listen to your vision, your lifestyle, and your home then we bring it to life with the finest fabrics, hand-tied springs, and meticulous stitching. Whether you're restoring a beloved antique or commissioning something entirely new, your satisfaction is guaranteed.

From residential homes to hospitality spaces, our work speaks for itself. Come see why hundreds of families have trusted us with their most treasured pieces.
